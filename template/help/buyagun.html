One of the most common ways to buy a gun is through a registered firearms dealer. The process typically works like this:
  1. You find a dealer who can sell you the gun you'd like to buy.
  2. You pay the dealer a deposit.
  3. The dealer gives you the serial number of the gun.
  4. You apply for a firearm certificate.
  5. The firearm certificate is issued.
  6. You show the firearm certificate to the dealer and pay the remainder of the price.
  7. The dealer records the sale and gives you the gun.
